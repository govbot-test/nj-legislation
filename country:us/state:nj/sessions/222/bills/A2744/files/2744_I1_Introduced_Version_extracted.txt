Section 1:
A2744 ASSEMBLY, No. 2744 STATE OF NEW JERSEY 222nd LEGISLATURE PRE-FILED FOR INTRODUCTION IN THE 2026 SESSION Sponsored by: Assemblywoman ANDREA KATZ District 8 (Atlantic and Burlington) SYNOPSIS Requires prescriptions for certain patients to include patients' weight. CURRENT VERSION OF TEXT Introduced Pending Technical Review by Legislative Counsel. An Act concerning prescriptions for medication and supplementing Title 45 of the Revised Statues. Be It Enacted by the Senate and General Assembly of the State of New Jersey: 1.  Notwithstanding the provision of any law, regulation, rule, or order to the contrary, any prescription or order for medication issued by an authorized prescriber for a patient, who is 18 years of age or younger, shall include the patients weight expressed in kilograms on the prescription or order. 2. This act shall take effect 180 days after the date of enactment. STATEMENT This bill provides that any prescription or order for medication issued by an authorized prescriber for a patient, who is 18 years of age or younger, is to include the patients weight expressed in kilograms on the prescription or order.
