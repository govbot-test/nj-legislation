STATEMENT

 

       This bill makes a supplemental appropriation of $750,000 from the General Fund to the Victims of Crime Compensation Office (VCCO) in the Department of Law and Public Safety for a public awareness campaign to inform victims of the provisions of the Crime Victims Bill of Rights and of the availability of compensation and other assistance from VCCO.
