SYNOPSIS

     Prohibits EDA from issuing subsidies for wind energy projects.

 

CURRENT VERSION OF TEXT

     Introduced Pending Technical Review by Legislative Counsel.

  

An Act prohibiting New Jersey Economic Development Authority subsidies for certain wind energy projects, supplementing Title 34 of the Revised Statutes, amending various parts of the statutory law, and repealing section 6 of P.L.2010, c.57.

 

     Be It Enacted by the Senate and General Assembly of the State of New Jersey:

 

     1.    (New section) Notwithstanding any other law, rule, or regulation to the contrary, beginning on the effective date of P.L.    , c.     (pending before the Legislature as this bill), the authority shall not issue any form of financial assistance, including grants, loans, loan guarantees, tax credits, or other subsidies, to support the development or operation of a wind energy project.

STATEMENT

 

     This bill prohibits the New Jersey Economic Development Authority (authority) from issuing any form of financial assistance, including grants, loans, loan guarantees, tax credits, or other subsidies, to support the development or operation of a wind energy project.

     Specifically, the bill amends existing law governing the following programs to remove eligibility for wind energy projects:  (1) the Global Warming Solutions Fund; (2) the Emerge Program; (3) the Garden State C-PACE program; and (4) the New Jersey Angel Investor Tax Credit program.  The bill also provides that the authority may not provide other forms of financial assistance to support the development or operation of a wind energy project.

     The bill also repeals a statute that currently provides for the authority to issue tax credits for offshore wind projects, including awards of certain unused Emerge Program or New Jersey Aspire Program tax credits for this purpose.
