A290 ASSEMBLY, No. 290 STATE OF NEW JERSEY 222nd LEGISLATURE PRE-FILED FOR INTRODUCTION IN THE 2026 SESSION Sponsored by: Assemblyman PAUL KANITRA District 10 (Monmouth and Ocean) Assemblyman GREGORY P. MCGUCKIN District 10 (Monmouth and Ocean) Co-Sponsored by: Assemblyman Inganamort and Assemblywoman Fantasia SYNOPSIS Requires AG to create voting integrity task force. CURRENT VERSION OF TEXT Introduced Pending Technical Review by Legislative Counsel. An Act concerning voting integrity and supplementing Title 52 of the Revised Statutes. Be It Enacted by the Senate and General Assembly of the State of New Jersey: 1.    The Attorney General shall create a voting integrity task force in the Division of Criminal Justice, Specialized Crimes Bureau to investigate and prosecute crimes involving voting fraud. The task force shall be comprised of members of State, county, and municipal law enforcement agencies, as determined by the Attorney General, and shall utilize a coordinated law enforcement strategy to uphold voting integrity in this State. 2.    This act shall take effect on the first day of the sixth month next following enactment. STATEMENT This bill requires the Attorney General to create a voting integrity task force in the Division of Criminal Justice, Specialized Crimes Bureau.  The purpose of the task force is to investigate and prosecute crimes involving voting fraud. Under the bill, the task force is to be comprised of members of State, county, and municipal law enforcement agencies, as determined by the Attorney General, and is to utilize a coordinated law enforcement strategy to uphold voting integrity in this State.
