STATEMENT

 

     This bill permits an order of payment by the Victims of Crime Compensation Office (VCCO) to individuals who have received forensic medical attention by a county sexual assault response team for an unreported sexual offense. 

     Under current law, an application for payment of compensation from the VCCO requires the applicant to have reported the offense to the police or other appropriate law enforcement agency.  For an applicant who has received forensic medical services from a sexual assault response team established pursuant to P.L.2001, c.81, (C.52:4B-54) for aggravated sexual assault, sexual assault, aggravated criminal sexual contact, or criminal sexual contact, the bill removes the requirement of a report to law enforcement.
