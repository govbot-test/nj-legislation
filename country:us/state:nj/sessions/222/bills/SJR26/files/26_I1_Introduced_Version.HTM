SYNOPSIS

     Designates May of each year as Military Spouse Appreciation Month in New Jersey.

 

CURRENT VERSION OF TEXT

     Introduced Pending Technical Review by Legislative Counsel.

  

A Joint Resolution designating May of each year as Military Spouse Appreciation Month in New Jersey. 

 

Whereas, The men and women in the Armed Forces courageously step up to defend our Nation and State, and keep its citizens safe; and

Whereas, As these servicemembers make heroic sacrifices for their country, their husbands and wives are often left to deal with the strains of relocation and deployment; and

Whereas, Though most military spouses do not wear a uniform themselves, they serve and strengthen our Nation and State every day by providing our brave troops with support, comfort, and love; and

Whereas, Military spouses are the rock on which their families, our military community, and our security depend; and

Whereas, They are silent heroes who provide the critical contributions of understanding and encouragement to our servicemembers, something that should be acknowledged and celebrated; and

Whereas, They deserve recognition for the contributions and sacrifices they make; and

Whereas, This State recognizes military spouses for their selfless and extraordinary service, sacrifices, strength, fortitude, and courage; and

Whereas, The State of New Jersey designates May of each year as Military Spouse Appreciation Month in New Jersey; now, therefore,

 

     Be It Resolved by the Senate and General Assembly of the State of New Jersey:

 

     1.  a.  The month of May of each year is designated as Military Spouse Appreciation Month in New Jersey.

     b.  The Governor is respectfully requested to annually issue a proclamation calling upon public officials and citizens of the State to observe the month with appropriate activities and programs.

 

     2.  This joint resolution shall take effect immediately.

 

 

STATEMENT

 

     This joint resolution designates May of each year as Military Spouse Appreciation Month. 

     The men and women in the Armed Forces courageously defend our Nation and State, and keep its citizens safe.  As they make heroic sacrifices for their county, their husbands and wives are often left to deal with the strains of relocation and deployment. 

     Though most military spouses do not wear a uniform themselves, they serve and strengthen our Nation and State every day be providing our brave troops with support, comfort, and love.  They are the rock on which their families, our military community, and our security depend.  They deserve recognition for the contributions and sacrifices they make.

     This State recognizes the selfless and extraordinary service and sacrifices of military spouses, and designates May of each year as Military Spouse Appreciation Month in New Jersey.
