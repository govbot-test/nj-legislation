STATEMENT

 

     This bill appropriates $350,000 from the General Fund to the Department of State for the Center for American Women and Politics  Women Elected and Appointed Officials Database.  P.L.2021, c.414 requires the Center for Women and American Politics (CAWP) to establish databases of elected and appointed officials in New Jersey.

     The CAWP, a unit of the Eagleton Institute of Politics at Rutgers, The State University of New Jersey, provides scholarly research and data about womens political participation in the United States.  CAWPs Women Elected Officials Database includes full historical listings for women who have held office at the congressional, State executive, and State legislative levels nationwide.
