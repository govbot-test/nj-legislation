A2161 ASSEMBLY, No. 2161 STATE OF NEW JERSEY 222nd LEGISLATURE PRE-FILED FOR INTRODUCTION IN THE 2026 SESSION Sponsored by: Assemblywoman ELIANA PINTOR MARIN District 29 (Essex and Hudson) Assemblywoman  VERLINA REYNOLDS-JACKSON District 15 (Hunterdon and Mercer) Assemblyman  CRAIG J. COUGHLIN District 19 (Middlesex) Co-Sponsored by: Assemblywomen Dunn, Haider, Park, Peterpaul and Donlon SYNOPSIS Makes supplemental appropriation of $615,000 to DHS for grant to NJ 2-1-1 Partnership. CURRENT VERSION OF TEXT Introduced Pending Technical Review by Legislative Counsel. A Supplement to the annual appropriations act for the fiscal year ending June 30, 2026, P.L.2025, c.74. Be It Enacted by the Senate and the General Assembly of the State of New Jersey: 1.    In addition to the amounts appropriated under P.L.2025, c.74, the annual appropriations act for the fiscal year ending June 30, 2026, there is appropriated from the General Fund the following amount for the purpose specified: 54 DEPARTMENT OF HUMAN SERVICES 20 Physical and Mental Health 23 Behavioral Health Services 7700 Division of Mental Health and Addiction Services GRANTS-IN-AID 08-7700  Community Services .............................................. $615,000 Total Grants-In-Aid appropriation, Division of Mental Health and Addiction Services $615,000 Grants-in-Aid: 08   NJ 2-1-1 Partnership Operating Aid....... ($615,000) 2.    This act shall take effect immediately. STATEMENT This bill appropriates $615,000 to the Department of Human Services for the purpose of awarding a grant to the NJ 2-1-1 Partnership Operating Aid. In June 2000, the Federal Communications Commission (FCC) authorized the designation of 2-1-1 for human services information and referral programs on a nationwide basis.  The FCC, in establishing the 2-1-1 designation, granted community groups the ability to establish the delivery of information and referral services using this universal access number on a state-by-state basis. In 2002, the New Jersey Board of Public Utilities (BPU) designated NJ 2-1-1 Partnership as the sole administrator of the 211 dialing code in New Jersey.  Since then, NJ 2-1-1 Partnership has been providing the 2-1-1 service Statewide.
