SYNOPSIS

     Allows customer choice of NJ E-ZPass automatic replenishment amount.

 

CURRENT VERSION OF TEXT

     Introduced Pending Technical Review by Legislative Counsel.

  


An Act allowing customer choice of automatic replenishment amount on New Jersey electronic toll collection system and supplementing Title 27 of the Revised Statutes.

 

     Be It Enacted by the Senate and General Assembly of the State of New Jersey:

 

     1.    a. Notwithstanding any law, rule, or regulation to the contrary, the New Jersey Turnpike Authority shall not enter into or renew a contract for administration of the electronic toll collection system unless that contract includes a process to provide a customer the option to select an automatic replenishment amount and change the automatic replenishment amount at any time.  This process shall, at minimum, provide a customer the option to select from multiple pre-determined automatic replenishment amounts or to choose the customers own automatic replenishment amount.

     b.    The New Jersey Turnpike Authority shall publish, on its Internet website, information concerning a customers ability to choose and change an automatic replenishment amount pursuant to subsection a. of this section.

 

     2.    a. Notwithstanding any law, rule, or regulation to the contrary, the South Jersey Transportation Authority shall not enter into or renew a contract for administration of the electronic toll collection system unless that contract includes a process to provide a customer the option to select an automatic replenishment amount and change the automatic replenishment amount at any time.  This process shall, at minimum, provide a customer the option to select from multiple pre-determined automatic replenishment amounts or to choose the customers own automatic replenishment amount.

     b.    The South Jersey Transportation Authority shall publish, on its Internet website, information concerning a customers ability to choose and change an automatic replenishment amount pursuant to subsection a. of this section.

 

     3.    This act shall take effect immediately but the provisions of P.L.    , c.     (C.        ) (pending before the Legislature as this bill) shall not be construed as affecting the terms of any contract or agreement in effect as of the effective date of P.L.    , c.     (C.        ) (pending before the Legislature as this bill).

 

 

STATEMENT

 

     This bill requires any contract entered into or renewed between an administrator of electronic toll collection and the New Jersey Turnpike Authority (NJTA) or the South Jersey Transportation Authority (SJTA) to include an option for a customer to select an automatic replenishment amount.  Under this bill, the NJTA and SJTA are required to ensure this option is available.  A New Jersey E-ZPass customer is to have the choice to select from multiple pre-determined automatic replenishment amounts for an individual account or to choose the customers own automatic replenishment amount.  The customer is also to have the ability to change the automatic replenishment amount at any time.

     NJTA and SJTA are required to publish information concerning a customers ability to choose and change an automatic replenishment amount on the authorities websites.
