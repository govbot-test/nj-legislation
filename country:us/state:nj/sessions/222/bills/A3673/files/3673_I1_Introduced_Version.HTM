STATEMENT

 

Â Â Â Â  This bill increases the hourly limit of reimbursable personal care assistant services under the NJ WorkAbility Program from 40 hours per calendar workweek to 112 hours per calendar workweek. The NJ WorkAbility Program provides full State Medicaid health coverage to individuals with disabilities who are working and whose earnings would otherwise make them ineligible for Medicaid.Â  The regulations (N.J.A.C.10:72-9 et seq.) governing the Program provide that all services available through the NJ WorkAbility Program must be provided through existing Medicaid contracts held by the Division of Medical Assistance and Health Services (DMAHS) in the Department of Human Services. Â Currently, the reimbursement of personal care assistant services under such contracts is limited to 40 hours per calendar work week.Â
