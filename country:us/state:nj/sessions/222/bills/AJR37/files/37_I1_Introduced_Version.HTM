Whereas, Sikhs celebrate Vaisakhi on the first day of the month of Vaisakh in the Sikh calendar, which typically falls on April 13th or 14th; and

Whereas, Vaisakhi is often celebrated by Sikhs attending their local house of worship known as a gurdwara to worship, meditate, and to participate in langar, which is a free community meal prepared and served by volunteers; and

Whereas, Sikhs celebrate Vaisakhi across New Jersey with festivals showcasing cultural performances, music, and traditional food; and
