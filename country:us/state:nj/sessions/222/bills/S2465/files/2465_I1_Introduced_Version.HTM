An Act designating State Highway Route 10 in honor of members of the American Legion.

 

Whereas, The American Legion is an organization of Unites States war veterans that was chartered by Congress in 1919 as a patriotic, war-time veterans service organization; and

Whereas, The American Legion evolved from a group of veterans of World War I and now has over 2,000,000 members and more than 13,000 posts worldwide; and

Whereas, The American Legion focuses on service to veterans, service members, and communities and has influenced considerable social change in the United States; and

Whereas, Since its inception, the American Legion has spearheaded numerous advocacy initiatives addressing critical issues impacting veterans and service members and has secured hundreds of benefits for veterans, service members, and their families; and

Whereas, In New Jersey, the American Legion has over 30,000 members and over 250 posts; and
