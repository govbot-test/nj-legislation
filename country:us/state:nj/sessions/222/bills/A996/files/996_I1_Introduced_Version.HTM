SYNOPSIS

     Requires health insurers to provide coverage for testing for suspected dyslexia.

An Act requiring insurance coverage for exams for dyslexia and supplementing various parts of the statutory law.

STATEMENT

 

     This bill requires health insurers (including insurance companies, hospital service corporations, medical service corporations, health service corporations, health maintenance organizations authorized to issue health benefits plans in New Jersey, and entities contracted to administer health benefits in connection with the State Health Benefits Program and School Employees Health Benefits Program) to provide coverage for testing for suspected dyslexia.

     Specifically, the bill requires coverage for suspected dyslexia that may not exclude coverage for the screening, diagnosis, or treatment of medical conditions otherwise covered by the policy.  Coverage is to include comprehensive neuropsychological examinations for the purposes of diagnosing dyslexia and determining the psychological, emotional, and educational wellness needs of the individual tested.
