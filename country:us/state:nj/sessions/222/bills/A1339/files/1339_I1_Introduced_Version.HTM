STATEMENT

 

Â Â Â Â  This bill increases the tax rate on fire insurance premiums collected by out-of-state insurers from two percent to three percent.

Â Â Â Â  Under current law, insurance companies not organized under New Jersey law that write fire insurance policies on New Jersey properties are required to pay a tax on two percent of the premiums received from such policies.Â  Similarly, New Jersey-based agents, brokers, or insurers who place fire insurance with out-of-state companies are also required to pay tax on two percent of these premiums.Â  In both cases, the tax is paid to the Treasurer of the New Jersey State Firemen's Association.
