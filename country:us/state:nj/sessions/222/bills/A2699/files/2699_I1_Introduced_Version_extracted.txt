A2699 ASSEMBLY, No. 2699 STATE OF NEW JERSEY 222nd LEGISLATURE PRE-FILED FOR INTRODUCTION IN THE 2026 SESSION Sponsored by: Assemblyman CHRISTOPHER P. DEPHILLIPS District 40 (Bergen, Essex and Passaic) SYNOPSIS Requires NJT to rescind certain fare increases. CURRENT VERSION OF TEXT Introduced Pending Technical Review by Legislative Counsel. An Act concerning New Jersey Transit Corporation fare increases. Be It Enacted by the Senate and General Assembly of the State of New Jersey: 1.    The New Jersey Transit Corporation shall rescind the fare increase of three percent, effective July 1, 2025, approved and authorized by the board pursuant to the April 15, 2024 board meeting minutes. 2.    This act shall take effect immediately. STATEMENT This bill requires the New Jersey Transit Corporation (corporation) to rescind the fare increase of three percent, effective July 1, 2025, approved and authorized by the corporations board pursuant to the April 15, 2024 board meeting minutes.
