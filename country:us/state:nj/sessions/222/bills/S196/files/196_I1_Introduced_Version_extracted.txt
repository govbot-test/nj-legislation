Section 1:
S196 SENATE, No. 196 STATE OF NEW JERSEY 222nd LEGISLATURE PRE-FILED FOR INTRODUCTION IN THE 2026 SESSION Sponsored by: Senator PATRICK J. DIEGNAN, JR. District 18 (Middlesex) Co-Sponsored by: Senator Burgess SYNOPSIS Prohibits certain animal adoption centers from denying applicant for adoption of dog or cat solely due to applicant's age. CURRENT VERSION OF TEXT Introduced Pending Technical Review by Legislative Counsel. An Act concerning the adoption of dogs and cats and supplementing Title 4 of the Revised Statutes. Be It Enacted by the Senate and General Assembly of the State of New Jersey: 1.  a.  As used in this section: Animal rescue organization means the same as the term is defined in section 1 of P.L.1941, c.151 (C.4:19-15.1). Foster home means the same as the term is defined in section 1 of P.L.1941, c.151 (C.4:19-15.1). Pound means the same as the term is defined in section 1 of P.L.1941, c.151 (C.4:19-15.1). Shelter means the same as the term is defined in section 1 of P.L.1941, c.151 (C.4:19-15.1). b.  Notwithstanding the provisions of any law, regulation, or rule to the contrary, no animal rescue organization, shelter, pound, or foster home shall deny an applicant for the adoption of a dog or cat solely on the basis of the applicants age. c.  Every animal rescue organization, shelter, pound, or foster home shall ensure that the animal rescue organization, shelter, pound, or foster homes adoption eligibility criteria for the adoption of a dog or cat is in compliance with the requirements of subsection b. of this section. 2.  This act shall take effect immediately. STATEMENT This bill would prohibit animal rescue organizations, shelters, pounds, and foster homes from denying an applicant for the adoption of a dog or cat solely on the basis of the applicants age.  The bill would also require every animal rescue organization, shelter, pound, or foster home to ensure that the animal rescue organization, shelter, pound, or foster homes adoption eligibility criteria for the adoption of a dog or cat is in compliance with the bills requirements.
